About Me:

Born and raised in Warsaw, Poland , a city as tired as me. Soon enough I moved to California and attended the "University of California, Berkeley" where I studied Economics and Political Economies (separate majors!). After a brief stint in financial litigation consulting I ventured into the world of Public Policy at the McCourt School of Public Policy.

There I learned key skills of Data Analysis , Science , and Visualization. I also improved on my econometric abilities. As my final project I wrote a paper on the relationship between rice commodity futures and local rice prices in low-income highly vulnerable nations called Rice, Futures, and Rice Bowls

In my spare time I write for the California Golden Blogs under the name Piotr T Le on the topic of sports, advanced statistics, and analysis.
